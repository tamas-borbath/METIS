The data contained in this folder relates to the 2050 scenario that was created in the context of the METIS study S1.

A description of the scenario may be found in the respective report of study S1 (Optimal flexibility portfolios for a high-RES 2050 scenario): https://op.europa.eu/en/publication-detail/-/publication/f26e4340-67fd-11e9-9f05-01aa75ed71a1/language-en/format-PDF/source-96288622

Further assessments of this scenario are integrated in the report of study S14 (Electricity prices and investor revenue risks in a high RES scenario): https://op.europa.eu/en/publication-detail/-/publication/bc0a0d63-6d4f-11e9-9f05-01aa75ed71a1/language-en/format-PDF/source-96288622
